Personal information:

What is Books & Beyond?

Some say it's a bookstore. Some call it a music shop, while others think of it as a great place to meet and relax. Books & Beyond has become a unique phenomenon that offers this area's largest selection of sale books and music, with an inventory of CDs and tapes ranging from classical to independent-label rock and everything in between.

What makes us different is our atmosphere. We encourage you to relax with a book in one of our quiet rooms, or take some reading material into our Coffee and Juice Bar to read while snacking. Think of Books & Beyond as your own den.

Why go to any other book store?

Need some quiet time away from your home or office? Need to put the speed and chaos of modern life in the backseat for a while? Sometimes, a little solitude buried in the pages of a good book is all you'll need. Visit with a friend over a super large double cappuccino or a fruit smoothie. Take a break from the everyday routine and pick up one of our award-winning desserts. You can do all these things at Books & Beyond.
